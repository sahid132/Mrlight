Our Story

Established in 2004 in the UAE, Mr. LIGHT is an international presence today in the Home Appliances market with operations spread across 26 countries. We launched ourselves initially with an impressive range of intelligently designed user friendly & affordable Torches & Emergency Lights. The rousing reception we received at the market and our passion for de-sign encouraged us to innovate across a vast variety of Home Appliances products . As a result, Mr.LIGHT has diversified into Five new & powerful Brands, each offering a mind boggling array of consumer choices in terms of products. We are proud that each of our brands are renowned as exemplary per-formers in their respective segments.

Our Approach

Our customers are our best Brand Ambassadors.

Why So?

At Mr. LIGHT, we believe our success comes from our practical, down- to- earth, finger-on- the-pulse approach to identifying genuine user needs and taking a passionate problem solving approach to Design . Our drive for excellence is engined by an inspired & well experienced international R&D team . Another huge plus factor is Mr Care ,the dedicated after-purchase service we offer. We have talented, friendly customer representatives at your service, no matter where you are located across the 26 countries where we have our presence. Setting the benchmark high on customer care, we also have an efficient fleet of Customer Service Vehicles - ‘Service on Wheels’, which provides service to customers in their own locality proving without doubt our reliability and capability to deliver ensure 100% customer satisfaction.

Our Team

At Mr. LIGHT with over 500 dedicated employees and dealers all over the world Team Play & Loyalty are our greatest strengths. An international company, our efficient vertically integrated operations, starting from raw materials right through to manufacturing, management and customer satisfaction, ensures that we create value at much faster rate than our competitors . This definitely tells positively on our Corporate Worth.

Our Worth

At Mr. LIGHT we enforce high standards of safety and environmental awareness in all our operations. We exercise constant vigilance in ensuring our commercial goals are al-ways aligned correctly with high ethical standards. While we are committed to delivering the best possible products to our customers we are just as committed to our stakeholders to deliver maxi-mum rewards at minimal risk. We have our annual revenues crossing $200 million, and the quarterly growth is estimated to be around 20 - 30%. Currently, Mr. LIGHT has a market share of 35 - 40%, which is a huge achievement and proof as to customer preference and loyalty our products.
